For regular discussions of the latest data science topics, you can go to the Not So Standard Deviations podcast that is co-hosted by myself and Dr. Hilary Parker , a Senior Data Analyst at Etsy. If you're an avid podcast listener, you can subscribe to the podcast through iTunes or any popular podcasting app.
